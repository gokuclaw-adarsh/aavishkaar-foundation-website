# Five Pronged Intervention Strategy

> The Foundation's core approach to building entrepreneurial ecosystems

---

## Overview

The Five Pronged Intervention Strategy is aligned with the Foundation's larger objective of **'Economic Entrepreneurship to Social Equity'**.

The ethos behind the initiative is rooted in Aavishkaar Foundation's overall mission to:
> "Build a 'Learning Ecosystem' to trigger entrepreneurial curiosity among youth in Uttar Pradesh"
