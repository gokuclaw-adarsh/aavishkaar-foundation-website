# Youth Entrepreneurship Program (YEP)

> Flagship program for schools and colleges

---

## Overview

The Youth Entrepreneurship Program (YEP) is Aavishkaar Foundation's flagship business idea challenge for Government Schools and Colleges. It aims to inculcate entrepreneurial curiosity amongst youth and lay the founding blocks towards a thriving ecosystem in Eastern UP.

---

## Format

1. **Entrepreneurship Development Workshops** conducted in schools/colleges
2. **Business Plan Challenge** launched
3. **Mentor Support** during ideation phase
4. **Finalist Presentations** to esteemed jury
5. **Awards Ceremony** with cash prizes
