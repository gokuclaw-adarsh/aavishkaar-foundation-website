# Entrepreneur Investing Program (EIP)

> Support for local and regional startups

---

## Overview

The Entrepreneur Investing Program (EIP), also known as the Entrepreneur Mentorship Programme (EMP), supports local and regional startups from across Uttar Pradesh. It provides mentorship, exposure, and funding to promising entrepreneurs.

---

## Categories

### Existing Entrepreneur
For registered businesses looking to scale

### Budding Entrepreneur
For business ideas ready to launch

### Special Category
For high-impact social enterprises

---
