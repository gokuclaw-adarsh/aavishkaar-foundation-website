## Event Details

**Dates:** April 12-13, 2024
**Venue:** Jagriti Enterprise Centre – Purvanchal (JECP), Deoria
**Edition:** 3rd YEP Initiative + 1st Entrepreneur Mentorship Programme

---

## About the Programme

The 3rd leg of Aavishkaar Foundation's initiative included:
1. **Young Entrepreneurship Programme (YEP)** - for schools and colleges
2. **Entrepreneur Mentorship Programme (EMP)** - for local entrepreneurs

### Metrics
- **1,500 students** participated
- **10 Government Schools**
- **5 Government Colleges**
- **100 business plans** evaluated
- **15 finalist teams** presented
- **5 winning teams** (3 schools + 2 colleges)
- **16 entrepreneurs** pitched
- **7 entrepreneur winners**

### Total Prizes
- YEP: **₹5,50,000**
- EMP: **₹13,00,000**

## Quote

### Vineet Rai, Founder and Chairman, Aavishkaar Group
> "We are thrilled to announce the expansion of our initiative, the Youth Entrepreneurship Program, to Kushinagar and Deoria for its 3rd Edition. Recognizing the untapped potential of these regions, Aavishkaar Foundation is eager to extend the Group's global expertise in nurturing the startup ecosystem. We are also delighted to launch a unique Entrepreneur Mentorship Program which will recognise and reward local entrepreneurs. Our mission remains steadfast: to empower the youth of Purvanchal to embark on their entrepreneurial journeys. Through this expansion, we aim to catalyse transformative change and uplift the entire region, focussing especially on fostering women, youth and the economically disadvantaged as the next generation of regional entrepreneurs"

## The Way Forward

Aavishkaar Foundation's mission is to "Build a 'Learning Ecosystem' to trigger entrepreneurial curiosity among youth in Uttar Pradesh".

**Goals:**
- Take program to a **million school and college students** in Eastern UP
- Invest in **1000 entrepreneurs** from Purvanchal over the next 5 years
- Bring flagship "Sankalp Forum" to Varanasi and Eastern UP
