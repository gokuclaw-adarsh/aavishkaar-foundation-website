> Source: https://aavishkaarfoundation.in/ghazipur-event/
> Date: August - November 2023

---

## Event Details

**Date:** November 4, 2023
**Venue:** The Grand Palace Hotel, Ghazipur
**Edition:** 2nd YEP Initiative

---

## About the Programme

With the vision to inculcate entrepreneurial curiosity amongst the youth and lay the founding blocks towards a thriving ecosystem in Eastern UP, the Young Entrepreneurship Program saw participation from:

- **3,285 students**
- **10 Government Schools**
- **6 Government Colleges**
- **17 finalists** presented their ideas
- **6 winning teams** (3 schools + 3 colleges)
