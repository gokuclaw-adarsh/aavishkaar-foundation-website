## Event Details

**Dates:** November 26-27, 2024
**Venue:** Rudraksh International Cooperation and Convention Centre, Varanasi
**Edition:** 1st Sankalp Bharat Summit

---

## About the Summit

**India's largest showcase of Entrepreneurship**

A celebration of impact startups from India, the Summit was the largest showcase of home grown innovators featuring:
- Renowned Business Leaders
- Entrepreneur Spotlights
- Curated Sessions
- Global Partners
- Exhibition Showcases
- Workshop & Master classes
- Entrepreneur Awards
- LIVE Deal Rooms
- Networking opportunities

### Metrics
- **700+ delegates**
- **6 districts** covered (Sept-Nov 2024)
- **12 student teams** rewarded
- **15 entrepreneurs** rewarded

## Programs Showcased

### Youth Entrepreneurship Program (YEP)
Business idea challenge from startup mentoring workshops conducted in Govt. Schools and Colleges across 6 districts in UP. Over **12 student teams** with promising startup ideas were rewarded.

### Entrepreneur Investing Program (EIP)
Featured local and regional startups across two categories:
- Registered Businesses
- Business Ideas

Over **15 entrepreneurs** with transformative ideas were rewarded.
