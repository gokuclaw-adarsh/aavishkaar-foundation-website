## Event Details

**Dates:** December 11-12, 2025
**Venues:**
- Foundation Day (Dec 11): Awadh Shilpgram
- Summit Day (Dec 12): Clark Avadh, Lucknow
**Edition:** 2nd Sankalp Bharat Summit

**Led by:** Aavishkaar Group Co-Founders Swati Rai and Vineet Rai

---

## About the Summit

The 2nd Edition of the Sankalp Bharat Summit 2025 was a celebration of impact startups from India and one of the largest showcases of home grown innovators featuring:
- Renowned Business Leaders
- Entrepreneur Spotlights
- Curated Sessions
- Global Partners
- Exhibition Showcases
- Workshop & Master classes
- Entrepreneur Awards
- LIVE Deal Rooms
- Immense networking opportunities

### Metrics
- **800+ delegates**
- **21 districts** across Eastern UP
- **Total prizes: ₹52 Lakhs**

## Foundation Day Activities (Dec 11)

Student teams from Govt. Schools and Colleges gathered as part of the "YEP" program and local/regional startups gathered as part of the 'EIP' Program at Awadh Shilpgram. An esteemed jury reviewed the business ideas in this daylong celebration.

On Dec 12, all students and local entrepreneurs participated in the main Sankalp Bharat Summit 2025.
