---

## Event Details

**Date:** April 21, 2023
**Venue:** Utkarsh Auditorium, Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh
**Edition:** 1st YEP Initiative

---

## About the Programme

Between Jan – Apr 2023, as part of the Young Entrepreneurship Programme, we conducted an entrepreneurship development workshop and had launched a business plan challenge across government schools in Varanasi. Designed to inculcate entrepreneurial curiosity amongst youth and to take steps toward a thriving entrepreneurial ecosystem in Eastern UP, this pilot initiative saw participation from:

- **1,100 children**
- **11 Government Schools**
- **98 business ideas** designed and presented

---

**Goals:**
- Take this program to a **million school children** in Eastern UP
- Cover colleges as well
- Invest in **1000 entrepreneurs** from Purvanchal over the next 5 years
- Bring flagship "Sankalp Forum" to Varanasi and Eastern UP
