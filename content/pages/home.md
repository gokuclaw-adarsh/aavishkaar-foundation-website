## Hero Section

### Headline
Driving Transformative Impact Regionally Through **Entrepreneurship of Marginalized Youth**

### Body Copy
At Aavishkaar Foundation we believe that unleashing the bottled spirit of entrepreneurship among unemployed and marginalized youth will have transformative impact across low income regions of India and accelerate economic and social inclusion.

We believe that empowered, ethical, curious and morally trained youth entrepreneurs can deliver jobs, livelihoods and impact through business intervention. They can become catalysts for change, inspiring others to participate in alleviating poverty, reducing vulnerabilities and transforming the region's income equity and prosperity. The foundation pilot intervention is directed towards Eastern Uttar Pradesh.

---

## Five Pronged Intervention Strategy

### ENGAGE
Inculcate entrepreneurial curiosity in young students

### SUPPORT
Groom young adults, unemployed or marginalized youth on business planning

### CATALYZE
Mobilize risk capital to finance the business vision of young entrepreneurs with expectations of limited return

### INSPIRE
Build local ecosystems by creating a platform to showcase entrepreneurial talent and their ideas to the world

### INSTITUTIONALIZE
Bring all learning together over time, to launch an educational curriculum through a mass entrepreneurship University

## Monitoring, Measurement and Reporting

The objective of Aavishkaar Foundation is to create a vibrant and supportive entrepreneurial ecosystem in the marginalized states of India.

The foundation follows the Aavishkaar Impact Measurement and Monitoring Framework which includes benchmarking at the outset, a projection of the estimated impact of the intervention, and finally an annual report on actual impact outputs and outcomes.

**Report:** [Aavishkaar Group Impact Report 2020](https://aavishkaargroup.com/wp-content/uploads/Aavishkaar_Impact_Report2020.pdf) - showcases the journey of 12 bold, resilient women who embraced risks to impact their communities.
