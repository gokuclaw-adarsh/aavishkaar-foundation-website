## About Aavishkaar Foundation

Aavishkaar Foundation was conceptualized in 2020 during the peak of the Covid lockdown.

Aavishkaar Group was searching for a response to assist vulnerable communities and created the Aavishkaar Group Facility for Humane Action. Internal contributions from the Group companies helped support mass migration by providing **370,000 meals** with the assistance from on-ground Not for Profits. The foundation also supported Government hospitals in **7 districts across 3 states** of India with adequate Covid aid.

The Board of Aavishkaar Group saw a huge opportunity in formalizing the 'Facility for Humane Action' to extend our impact footprint beyond the for-profit approach to deliver social impact.

Aavishkaar Group's Facility for Humane Action was formalized into a not-for-profit initiative â€“ **Aavishkaar Foundation**.

The Foundation's mission is to support creation of an entrepreneurial ecosystem that inspires young minds to generate livelihoods, and/or reduce risk and vulnerabilities amongst the low income population. The Foundation's belief is that focused intervention in a concentrated geography would help build a thriving entrepreneurial ecosystem in shorter time and deliver measurable impact.

Given the vastness of this challenge, the foundation will focus on **Eastern Uttar Pradesh** in the immediate future.

## Our Journey (Timeline)

### April 2020
The Aavishkaar Group Facility for Humane Action was established to provide economic assistance to the most severely affected during Covid

### June - Aug 2020
The Aavishkaar Group Facility for Humane Action supported **16 Grantee Partners** across **30+ Districts** and **10 States** of India

### September 2020
Aavishkaar Foundation was born and **Mr. U.K. Sinha**, Former Chairman of SEBI was selected as the 1st Chairperson of the Foundation

### June 2021
The Foundation mobilised Oxygen concentrators and Masks to combat the pandemic across India

### July-Sept 2021
Partnered with TotalStart Entrepreneurship Ecosystem Developer to support artisan families

### Oct 2021
Distributed **5 lakh masks** to Arohan customers in North East India and West Bengal

### May-June 2022
Provided capacity building and training for Arohan customers and community members in Bihar and Jharkhand, including exposure visits and forward linkages to sell their produce in the market

### Aug 2022
Promoted livelihood initiatives of agriculture and backyard poultry for community members in West Bengal

### Sept 2022
Conducted capacity building and training for women in Bihar and Jharkhand
