## To Respond to Natural Calamities by Providing Relief Interventions at the Bottom of the Pyramid

At Aavishkaar Foundation while our focus is on unleashing entrepreneurship amongst the rural youth, we also engage in relief initiatives during natural calamities. We are currently supporting **4 relief initiatives** across Bihar, Maharashtra, West Bengal and Uttar Pradesh with the aim to strengthen local communities and provide basic resources.

---

## Relief Initiatives

### 1. Built a Community Kitchen with SSF Annapurna
Aavishkaar Foundation partnered with the Solapur Social Foundation's 'Annapurna Yojana' to build a large community kitchen.

### 2. Going To School
Aavishkaar Foundation has enabled kids from poorer backgrounds in states like Bengal/Bihar to empower them to develop novel solutions for the challenges they face.

### 3. Covid Response for Underserved Communities
Aavishkaar Foundation partnered with Anmol Garments, a registered MSME in Kotwa to manufacture PPE and masks.

### 4. TotalStart Partnership
Total Start has engaged with the Dokra (traditional Indus Valley metal craft artisans) community since 2014, helping Dokra artisans sell their wares to corporates.
